Biography Of Nikola Tesla

Early Life

nikola Tesla was born on July 10 1856 in Similjan which was part of Australian Empire(modern day Coratia)
His father was a priest and his mother was an inventor who encouraged his curiosity from young age

Career

Tesla moved to United States in 1884 to work for Thomas Edition he later invented the alternating current electrical system which laid the foundation for modern electricity distribution.
he also conducted pioneering experiments in wireless communication and X Rays.

Achievements

Tesla held over 300 patents during his lifetime he is best known for his contribution to development of ac power the Tesla Coil and the wire less technologies.
despite his many contributions tesla faced financial difficulties later in his life

legacy

Tesla's works has left a lasting impact on modern technology as invasions have waved the way for many of electrical systems we use today.
he is remember as the visionary who was ahead of his time.
